Why does this exist?

Hi, I’m Paul. I live in Minneapolis and like participating in Restaurant Week. What I didn’t like was the questionable UI/UX experience on the canonical mspmag.com website where results were paginated, menus opened in modals, and it was generally difficult to browse & share.

So, I made this. It’s a simple website that scrapes the same data and displays it in a no-frills fashion. Read more.
